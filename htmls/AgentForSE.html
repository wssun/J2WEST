Agent For SE

    A significant innovation in this research is the construction of paired datasets comprising requirements, architecture, code, test/security, and verification data. This will address the current lack of high-quality training/learning data for LLMs, improving their ability to generate application-level code. By building a Multi-Agent Collaboration Platform, the research will provide a foundation for more scalable and modular automation in software engineering (SE).
